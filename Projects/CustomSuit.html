My custom suit project is a more personal project that is very long-term, but the end result is going to be a costume of my personal character I use.

The eyes are LED arrays with a Arduino controlling them. The head will consist of a one-way dark tinted visor so I can look out but nobody can see in. The body will consist of thin layers of foam covered with a smooth material.

Status: 3D Printer under repair
